ja tsön, a sensory journey through darkness

Nov 27, 2024

Our Story Menu Gallery

In Tibetan, ja tsön means "rainbow" — a spectrum of colors we cannot see in darkness, but can feel through taste. Here, we invite you to dine in complete darkness, allowing your other senses to guide you through a journey of flavors, textures, and emotions. Each dish tells a story of connection, each bite creates a moment of pure sensory exploration.
